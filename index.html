Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka has a rich and ancient history and is known for many things, but Sri Lanka is most famous for its incredible tea fields and production, abundant wildlife, lush jungles, beautiful beaches, historic Buddhist temples, precious gems, and varied landscapes & biodiversity. The smiles and hospitality of Sri Lanka are world famous and so are its spicy food, exotic fruits, and an array of sweetmeats found nowhere in the world.
